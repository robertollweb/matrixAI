# MatrixAI Business Model

MatrixAI core and CLI are free software released under the GNU Affero General Public License version 3 (AGPL v3). The license verification record is available in [LICENSE_VERIFICATION.md](LICENSE_VERIFICATION.md). The public core is intended to remain usable, auditable, and redistributable by the community.

## What Is Free

- Core language, runtime, CLI, training pipeline, registry, HTTP serving, deployment tools, and documentation.
- Public source code under AGPL v3.
- Community use, modification, redistribution, and contribution under the license terms.

## Future Commercial Layer

MatrixAI Studio as a polished downloadable product is planned after the v1.0 roadmap, only if real adoption shows demand for it. That future Studio is expected to communicate with the AGPL core over HTTP instead of importing Python modules directly.

The architecture is designed to keep the AGPL core and any future commercial Studio separate. The legal and commercial implications of that separation must be validated with a qualified professional before launching a paid product.

## Donations

Voluntary donations may be accepted through GitHub Sponsors or an equivalent platform to help cover hosting costs and development time. Donations are not required to use the core.

## Fiscal And Legal Notice

This document is a product decision record, not legal, tax, or accounting advice. Donation handling, commercial income, VAT/sales tax, and any future paid license terms must be reviewed with qualified professionals before launch.

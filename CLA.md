## How To Sign

You do not need to sign anything by hand. When you open your first pull
request, an automated check will ask you to confirm your acceptance of this
Agreement by posting a comment on the pull request with the exact text:

```
I have read the CLA Document and I hereby sign the CLA
```

Your GitHub username and the signing date are then recorded in
`.github/cla-signatures.json` as the record of your acceptance.
